HP Spectre

Specifications

HP Spectre has a 2.5GHz Intel Core i7-6500U dual-core 4MB Cache with Turbo Boost. It has a Intel HD Graphics 520. Also, it has 8GB RAM and a 13.3-inch 1920x1080 IPS WLED-backlit display. The HP Spectre has 256GB storage and 2 USB-C Thunderbolt 3 ports as well as a headphone jack. It has Bluetooth 4.0 and weighs 2.45 pounds.

Comparison

The HP Spectre has been an Apple Macbook Pro competitor since it went on sale as being the world's thinnest laptop. However, costing relatively the same as a Macbook Pro and not having specs near to the Macbook Pro, the HP comes just short to the Macbook Pro.

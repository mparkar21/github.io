Dell Precision 15 5000 Series

Specifications

The chassis has been completely redesigned to be 6%* thinner, 17%* smaller and under 4 pounds. The InfinityEdge display gives you a smaller bezel allowing for a 15.6” 4K Ultra HD viewing area in the size of some 14” notebooks. It comes in a range of Intel Core such as i7 and i5. It always comes with a Quad Core processor which allows multi-tasking. It has Windows 10 which is the latest operating system. It has a 32GB memory and a 15.6 UltraSharp FHD IPS Wide View Anti-Glare LED-backlit.

Comparison

The Dell Precision 15 5000 Series varies from £400 to £700. It has sold many units but not near to that of the Apple Macbook Pro.

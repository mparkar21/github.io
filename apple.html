Apple Macbook Pro

Specifications

The Apple Macbook Pro has a 12-inch display. It has a 2304x1440 resolution at 226 pixels per inch. Also, it has a range of other supported scaled resolutions such as 1440x900. The processor has a 1.2GHz dual-core Intel Core m3, Turbo Boost up to 3.0GHz, with 4MB L3 cache. You can also get a 1.3GHz dual core Intel Core i5 processor. It has 8GB of 1866MHz LPDDR3 onboard memory which is configurable to 16GB of memory. It comes with Wi-Fi and Bluetooth 4.2 wireless technology and supports USB-C. There is a full-sized keyboard with individual LED backlit keys and a trackpad.

Sales

The Apple Macbook Pro has been a proven success. Within the first 5 days of sales, the Apple Macbook Pro had outsold the total sales of every other Windows-powered laptop. The Apple Macbook pro had sold almost four times as many units as the Surface Book, nine times as many units as the Asus Chromebook Flip, and ten times the Lenovo Yoga 900.
